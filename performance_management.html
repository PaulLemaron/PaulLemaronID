Performance Management
Interactive Course

A self‑paced e‑learning module for 1,500+ healthcare staff
📌 Project Overview

Hospital, a 300‑bed faith‑based hospital in Kenya, needed a unified training resource for their revamped Performance Management System. I designed and developed a comprehensive, self‑paced interactive course covering the entire performance cycle – from goal setting to annual appraisal.

The course is deployed on Canvas LMS and is used by all staff (clinical, nursing, administration, support) to understand their roles, responsibilities, and the performance process.

📚

12 Modules

Covering Welcome, Governance, Performance Cycle, Goal Setting, Coaching, Reviews, PIPs, Culture, Support, and a Knowledge Check.

👥

1,500+ Staff

Target audience across all departments – clinical, nursing, administration, laboratory, pharmacy, and support services.

📱

Fully Responsive

Optimised for desktop, tablet, and mobile – accessible anytime, anywhere.

✅

Quiz + Certification

Integrated 5‑question knowledge check with an 80% passing threshold to ensure competency.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

HTML5 / CSS3 JavaScript Canvas LMS Canva ChatGPT (AI) ADDIE Framework Responsive Design WCAG Accessibility
